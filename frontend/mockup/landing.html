What ARIA Does

An end-to-end system that replaces manual underwriting review with a GenAI-powered pipeline.

Document Intelligence

Upload PDFs of policies, claims, or underwriting submissions. spaCy NER extracts risk entities — perils, coverage types, monetary values, and claim history.

Explainable Predictions

XGBoost predicts claim probability and severity. SHAP values explain exactly which factors drive the risk score — not a black box.

AI Narratives

Claude synthesizes predictions, SHAP factors, and similar historical cases into a structured underwriter narrative — ready for review.

The 4-Stage Pipeline

Each stage runs as an independent microservice, orchestrated by Docker Compose.

1

Ingestion + NER

PySpark ingests documents. spaCy NER with custom insurance patterns extracts risk entities.

PySpark spaCy FastAPI
2

Prediction

XGBoost scores claim probability and severity. SHAP explains feature contributions.

XGBoost SHAP
3

RAG Retrieval

Sentence-transformers embed entities. FAISS retrieves top-k similar cases from claims + SEC EDGAR filings.

FAISS MiniLM EDGAR
4

LLM Synthesis

Claude orchestrates via tool-use — calls prediction and RAG, then generates the underwriter narrative.
